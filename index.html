About NWUSA

NWUSA (Need Weed USA) is the safest way to discover 420 News, Updates Products and Events available in your area. NWUSA is only available to users who are +21 years of age and where purchase/ gifting of 420 items is legal. Download the app today and discover what's available near you!

How The App Works?

Download & Sign-Up

Use the links on this page to download the NWUSA App for Android or iOS and create an account to get started.

Placing an Order

Select a state and find a vendor with products available in your area. When you place an order you will get a text message to confirm.

Payment

NWUSA allows you to pay with Crypto, as well as using your bank or card for maximum flexibility.

Track Your Order

After your order is confirmed a driver is dispatched in your area and you will recieve a link to follow your driver along his route.
